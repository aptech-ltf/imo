Understanding SEO Basics

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) involves optimizing web content to rank higher on search engines like Google, improving visibility and traffic.

On-page SEO Factors

On-page SEO involves optimizing individual web pages to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic in search engines. This includes things like meta tags, headings, and keywords.

SEO Best Practices

Some of the best practices include optimizing images, using alt attributes, adding internal and external links, improving page speed, and making the site mobile-friendly.
